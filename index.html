Legacy

Lasting Reforms

Beyond warfare, Napoleon modernized France and much of Europe through comprehensive legal, educational, and administrative reforms.

Napoleonic Code

2,281 articles establishing equality before the law, property rights, and secular authority. Still influences 70+ countries today.

Education System

Established 45 lycées (secondary schools) and reorganized universities, creating the foundation of modern French education.

Bank of France

Founded in 1800 to stabilize currency and manage national finances. Still operates today.

Infrastructure

Built thousands of kilometers of roads and bridges across Europe, improving trade and communication.

1812 – 1821

Downfall

Napoleon's invasion of Russia in 1812 marked the beginning of his decline. Of 600,000 soldiers who entered Russia, only 100,000 returned.

1812 Disastrous Russian campaign – 83% casualties
1813 Defeated at Leipzig
1814 Exiled to Elba
1815 Escaped, ruled 100 days, defeated at Waterloo
1821 Died in exile on Saint Helena, age 51
