The Napoleonic Code

Perhaps his most lasting legacy: a comprehensive legal code that established equality before the law, property rights, and secular authority. It still influences legal systems in over 70 countries.

Modernizing France

Cultural Impact

He commissioned the Arc de Triomphe, reorganized the Louvre, and supported the arts. His reforms modernized administration throughout Europe, spreading principles of the French Revolution.

The Russian Disaster
